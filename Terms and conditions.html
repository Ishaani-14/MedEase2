There are the following terms and conditions



  • Donor
    • Eligibility: Donors must meet the eligibility criteria specified by the platform, which may include age, legal capacity, and other requirements.
    • Voluntary Participation: Donors participate in the platform voluntarily and are not coerced or compelled to make donations.
    • Accuracy of Information: Donors agree to provide accurate and truthful information about themselves and their donations.
    • Privacy and Confidentiality: Donors' personal information will be treated with confidentiality and will not be shared with third parties without their consent, except as required by law.

  • Needy
    • Verification of Needy Requests: Donors understand that the platform may verify the authenticity and legitimacy of needy requests before disbursing donations to ensure they reach genuinely deserving individuals or organizations.
    • Accountability and Reporting: Donors expect transparency and accountability from the platform regarding the utilization of donated funds or items. The platform agrees to provide regular updates or reports detailing how donations are being used and the impact they have made on the lives of the needy.
    • Code of Conduct: Donors agree to adhere to a code of conduct that promotes respectful and ethical interactions within the donor and needy community.
